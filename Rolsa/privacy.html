Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your personal information when you use our website and services related to consultations. By using our site you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Data We Collect

  • Account information — name, email address and password (hashed) you provide when creating an account.
  • Contact details — phone number and postal address when you choose to add them to your profile or consultation form.
  • Consultation records — the details you submit when requesting a consultation, including type, date and any reason or notes.
  • Technical information — IP address, browser type, operating system and cookies used to improve site functionality.

How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

  • Provide, operate and maintain our website and services.
  • Process and manage consultations you submit and keep you informed about status changes.
  • Authenticate accounts and secure access to account-only pages.
  • Respond to your requests and provide customer support.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, remember preferences, and analyse site usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some features.

Sharing and Disclosure

We will not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data with trusted third parties who provide services on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics) under contract and with appropriate safeguards. We may disclose information to comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical and physical measures to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Your Rights

You may access, correct or delete your personal information by logging into your account and editing your profile or by contacting us at the address below. We will respond to requests in accordance with applicable law.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@rolsa.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date will be updated at the top of the page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.
