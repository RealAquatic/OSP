How can i reduce my carbon footprint?

Tips to reduce your carbon footprint

  • Switch to energy-efficient LED lighting and smart thermostats.
  • Reduce car use: walk, cycle, use public transport or car-share where possible.
  • Improve home insulation to lower heating energy needs.
  • Choose low-carbon diets (reduce red meat consumption) and minimise food waste.
  • Buy durable, repairable products and favour sustainable suppliers.
