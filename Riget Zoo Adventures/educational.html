Educational Visits

Discover the wonders of wildlife with our tailored educational visits, perfect for schools,
clubs, and learning groups. Each visit includes a guided tour led by one of our experienced educators,
hands-on learning sessions with select ambassador animals, and access to our educational materials designed
to support curriculum topics such as habitats, conservation, and animal behavior.
To book an educational visit, please call our Education Team to schedule an appointment,
discuss group needs, and customize your experience.

Pricing (per student): Standard Educational Visit: £6 per student
Guided Tour + Animal Encounter Session: £15 per student
Full Educational Program (tour, workshop, and animal encounter): £25 per student
Teachers and chaperones enter free at a 1:10 ratio.
For availability and planning support, simply give us a call, we’re happy to help create a memorable learning adventure!
